# TriggerMap Privacy Policy

TriggerMap collects the information required to provide mood logging, weekly insight generation, authentication, subscriptions, analytics, and crash monitoring.

## Information we collect

- Account data such as name and email address when you register.
- Anonymous device identifiers used to preserve logs before sign-in.
- Trigger logs, emotions, notes, reminders, and exported history that you create in the app.
- Subscription purchase tokens and transaction metadata required for Google Play verification.
- Diagnostic and analytics events through Sentry and PostHog.

## How we use information

- To store and sync your TriggerMap journal entries.
- To generate weekly summaries and patterns from your own entries.
- To authenticate users and verify subscription access.
- To diagnose crashes, monitor service health, and improve app reliability.

## Sharing

We use trusted processors to operate the service, including Vercel, Upstash Redis, Google sign-in, Google Play subscription APIs, PostHog, and Sentry. We do not sell personal data.

## Retention and deletion

We retain data while your account is active or while anonymous logs remain linked to your device. You can request deletion or export by contacting support.

## Contact

For privacy requests, contact support@triggermap.app.
